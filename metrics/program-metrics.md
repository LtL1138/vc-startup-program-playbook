# Program Metrics (Adoption + Retention)

## North-star
Activated startups with 90-day retention.

## Adoption
- New enrollments
- Activation milestones (project created, auth enabled, first deploy)
- Time-to-first-success

## Retention and expansion signals
- Weekly active usage
- Increased projects/teams
- Support volume trending down
- Partner-sourced success stories

## Partner health
- Intros per quarter
- Portfolio participation rate
- Repeat programming attendance
