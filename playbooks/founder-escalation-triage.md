# Founder Escalation Triage

## Intake
Capture: issue summary, severity, business impact, deadline, screenshots/logs.

## Categorize
Auth, database, performance, billing/credits, product limitations, integrations.

## Route
- Support: standard issues
- Solutions/technical resources: architecture help
- Product/engineering: bugs, roadmap gaps

## Close loop
Confirm resolution, document root cause, and add prevention notes to the playbook.
