# Startup Program Ops (Scale Template)

## Goal
Make enrollment, benefits delivery, and success tracking scalable for thousands of startups.

## Enrollment flow
Eligibility → approval → onboarding → benefits activation → success check-ins

## Benefit tiers (example)
- Seed: credits + quickstart + community support
- Series A/B: higher credits + office hours + prioritized support
- Strategic: white-glove routing + solutions support

## Systems to track
- Startup info (stage, VC, region)
- Enrollment status
- Activation milestones
- Support tickets and escalation notes

## Operating cadence
Weekly program health review using adoption and retention metrics.
